# Account Login Issues

## I can't log in to my Yahoo Finance account

If you're having trouble signing in, try these steps:

1. Make sure you're using your full Yahoo email address (e.g. yourname@yahoo.com)
2. Check that Caps Lock is off
3. Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try again
4. Try signing in from a different browser or device

If you've forgotten your password, use the **Forgot password** link on the sign-in page to reset it via email or phone.

## My account is locked

Yahoo locks accounts after multiple failed sign-in attempts as a security measure. Wait 12 hours and try again, or contact support to unlock it sooner.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA) issues

If you're not receiving your 2FA code:
- Check that your phone number or authenticator app is correctly linked under **Account Security** settings
- Make sure you have cell service or Wi-Fi
- Request a new code — codes expire after 60 seconds

Still stuck? Visit [Yahoo Account Help](https://help.yahoo.com/kb/account) or reach out to our support team.
