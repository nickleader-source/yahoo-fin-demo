# Market Data and Quotes

## Why is my quote delayed?

Yahoo Finance provides:
- **Real-time quotes** for NYSE and NASDAQ stocks for signed-in users with a Yahoo Finance Plus subscription
- **15-minute delayed quotes** for free accounts

To get real-time data, upgrade to **Yahoo Finance Plus** under your account settings.

## What data sources does Yahoo Finance use?

Yahoo Finance sources data from:
- **ICE Data Services** for US equities
- **Morningstar** for mutual fund data
- **Refinitiv** for international markets
- **CryptoCompare** for cryptocurrency prices

## Why does my chart look different from another platform?

Minor differences between platforms are normal and can result from:
- Different data adjustment methods (splits, dividends)
- Different time zone handling for pre/after-market sessions
- Data source differences for OTC or international securities

## I see a price that looks wrong

If a quote appears significantly off from other sources, it may be a stale tick or a data feed interruption. Please report it using the **Flag** icon on the quote page and our data team will investigate.
