# Alerts and Notifications

## How do I set a price alert?

1. Go to the quote page for any stock
2. Click the **Bell** icon next to the ticker
3. Set your target price and choose alert direction (above or below)
4. Choose delivery: push notification, email, or both
5. Click **Save**

You can manage all active alerts under **My Portfolio > Alerts**.

## Why didn't I receive my alert?

Common reasons:
- Push notifications are disabled for Yahoo Finance in your phone settings
- The alert email went to your spam folder
- The price target was hit outside market hours and the alert fires on the next open

To check: go to **Settings > Notifications** and make sure alerts are enabled.

## How many alerts can I set?

- **Free accounts**: up to 25 active alerts
- **Yahoo Finance Plus**: unlimited alerts

## Can I set alerts for news or earnings?

Yes. On any stock's quote page, click **Follow** to receive:
- Breaking news alerts
- Earnings date reminders
- Analyst rating changes

These are separate from price alerts and count separately toward your limit.
