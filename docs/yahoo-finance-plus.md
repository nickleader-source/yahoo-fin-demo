# Yahoo Finance Plus

## What's included in Yahoo Finance Plus?

Yahoo Finance Plus gives you:
- Real-time streaming quotes (no 15-min delay)
- Advanced charting with 100+ technical indicators
- Fair value estimates and analyst price targets
- Portfolio analytics and risk scoring
- Ad-free experience
- Mobile app premium features

## How much does it cost?

| Plan | Price |
|---|---|
| Yahoo Finance Plus Essential | $24.99/month or $249.99/year |
| Yahoo Finance Plus Advanced | $39.99/month or $399.99/year |

Both plans include a 14-day free trial.

## How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Sign in to Yahoo Finance
2. Go to **Account Settings > Subscriptions**
3. Click **Manage** next to Yahoo Finance Plus
4. Select **Cancel subscription**

Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period. You won't be charged again after canceling.

## I was charged but I cancelled

If you believe you were incorrectly charged after canceling, contact our billing support team with your order confirmation number and we'll review the charge within 2 business days.
