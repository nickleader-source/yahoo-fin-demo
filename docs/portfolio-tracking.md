# Portfolio Tracking

## How do I add stocks to my portfolio?

1. Sign in to Yahoo Finance
2. Click **My Portfolio** in the top navigation
3. Select **Create portfolio** or open an existing one
4. Click **+** and search for a stock ticker (e.g. AAPL, TSLA)
5. Enter the number of shares and purchase price
6. Click **Add**

Your portfolio will now track the current value, daily change, and total return.

## How do I import a portfolio from another platform?

Yahoo Finance supports CSV import:
1. Export a CSV from your brokerage (must include ticker, shares, purchase price, date)
2. Go to **My Portfolio > Import**
3. Upload the CSV and map the columns

Supported brokerages for direct import: Fidelity, Schwab, TD Ameritrade, E*TRADE.

## Why is my portfolio value wrong?

Portfolio values update every 15 minutes during market hours using real-time data from major exchanges. After-hours prices are delayed. If a ticker shows N/A, the security may be delisted or the symbol entered incorrectly.

## Can Fin give me investment advice?

Fin can help with account questions, platform navigation, and general information about features. Fin does not provide personalized investment advice. For financial guidance, please consult a licensed financial advisor.
